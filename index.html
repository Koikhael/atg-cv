About us

Subcontract security specialists with proven event support capability.

Close Protection Security Services LTD is an active UK private limited company incorporated on 14 June 2023. We provide specialist staffing and operational support to security firms, venues and festival operators, offering reliable teams that integrate with existing operations and maintain a high standard of conduct.

Who we are

We source well-presented, internally vetted officers with backgrounds in close protection, door supervision and event security. Our workforce is selected for professional conduct, seamless integration and consistent delivery under live operational conditions.
